Performance & Stability Tips

  • Use a recent version of Forge or Fabric compatible with your game build.
  • Test new mods on a copy of your world first; add mods one at a time to spot conflicts.
  • Allocate reasonable RAM (e.g., 4–6 GB for modded play) and keep your GPU drivers up to date.
  • Pair with optimization mods (e.g., performance renderers) for smoother frames in decorated bases.
